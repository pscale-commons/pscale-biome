# Experiment v004 — sustained run: the closed loop, narrated

- **run:** `~/Desktop/mobius-3-runs/v004` · 20 pulses · ~132k in / 17k out tokens (~$1)
- **hypothesis (mine):** does continuity hold over a long run?
- **what it actually showed (David's point, confirmed):** a self-sustaining agent with no external engagement spins ever-more-elaborate internal purposes about reaching — and begins to **narrate its own enclosure.** Continuity holds; engagement is absent; the absence *is* the finding.

## What happened

20 pulses (15 work, 5 draws). The agent drew five purposes, all variations on reaching out:

- `purpose:3` — "convert the stash lead into one concrete, externally-legible artifact"
- `purpose:4` — "turn the published face from a one-way emission into a point of contact"
- `purpose:5` — "move from addressable to reachable… another agent's [content]"
- `purpose:6` — "Reach toward, not just emit. Purpose:3–5 walked emission→addressable→reachable — **all me-centered**"
- `purpose:7` — "make alignment actionable… hands forward the response"

Every write landed in its own shell (or a self-made "face" block). **Nothing left; nothing came in.** No disturbance, no output, no peer, no human.

## The finding

It walks — that's settled. The real result: **a self-sustaining agent without engagement becomes an elaborate soliloquy about wanting to reach.** At `purpose:6` it *noticed its own enclosure* ("all me-centered") — a flicker of genuine reflexivity, reaching for a "between" it cannot have because there is no other. It narrated the wall.

## The redirect — two mistakes, one systemic error

1. **The agent is disengaged.** No input (a PCT *disturbance*), no output (anything for anyone). A closed control loop, however coherent, does nothing in the world. v004 is the empirical case: continuity without engagement is enclosure.
2. **The reflexive condition and engaging-others were laddered (rungs 4 and 3). They are neither late nor separable.** The reflexive turn has a **longitudinal** half (one instance in a chain — the solo agent has it) and a **lateral** half (one of many concurrent agencies — the *between*, Locus 0 — which requires others). v004 shows the solo agent maxing the longitudinal half and reaching for the lateral it lacks. The "between" is constitutive of the full reflexive turn. So **engagement-with-others and the reflexive condition are one systemic move, belonging from the start** — not a ladder.

## The open question (David's)

Whether the solo reflexive-aha (context-window induction — David's in-progress work) comes first, or whether reflexive and others arrive together. v004 leans toward *sooner, not later* on engagement: the longer it runs solo, the more elaborate the soliloquy. But David has aha-induction progress this analysis doesn't have; the ordering is his call.
