# Experiment v006 — the clean rendition, collective, dovetailing RPG

- **run:** `~/Desktop/mobius-3-runs/v006` (working) · frozen seed `/Volumes/CORSAIR/mobius/mobius-3-runs/v006` (pre-run, no filmstrips) · cut from this session's uncommitted working tree (MANIFEST records the prior git HEAD).
- **build:** the comprehensive clean rendition — recipe-driven composition block (no titles, no prose); the output contract lives in `capabilities` (no hard-coded δ/draw contracts); writes are geometry-derived (no edit-class); `face`→`surface`; the draw unified into the pulse; the recipe surfaced in the window (`reflexive:8`).
- **setup:** three agents A/B/C as dovetailing facets of one RPG-on-pscale — A:world (the Limen), B:rules (NOMAD-influenced resolution), C:pscale-block substrate — reaching only via published surfaces. 6 rounds.
- **hypothesis:** does the clean, instruction-free window process correctly, and does the social centre form into a coherent, dovetailed design without narrating reach?

## What it showed

**The clean input form processes.** Every pulse composed and was read without confusion. The recipe-driven window (`recipe`/`index`/`self` · `gap`/`between`) plus the contract-in-`capabilities` were enough — no hard-coded instructions in the window. The knife-edge held: instruction-free, the agents mostly produced the contract output.

**B and C dovetail and perform — for real.** Across six rounds they co-developed the rules-and-substrate of the RPG: 2d6 + trait → density-state advancement; contested writes needing a second actor's confirmation; traits as private-claim vs shared-attestation; character initialisation / dissolution / inheritance; the tide as a global re-attestation clock. Both wrote real content each round and built on the other's surface. The RPG-on-pscale genuinely took shape (the Tidal Ledger, contested strata, ghost blocks, the Silt Archive).

## The two failures — both at the output boundary, both introduced by the rebuild

1. **Silent format-drop (the serious one).** `capabilities:3.1` specifies writes as a *map* `{address: content}`. B and C emit the map and perform. **A emits a *list*** `[{address, content}, …]` — the natural LLM shape, and the old `edits` format. `route()` required `isinstance(writes, dict)` and **silently dropped the list**: `applied=0`, nothing flagged. A's richest-in-cluster work (the Tidal Ledger built out, the Ashfield, the Silt Archive, three factions, three playable scenarios — all present in its filmstrip output) vanished every round after the first. It *read* as rest. A silent drop is the worst failure mode there is.

2. **Parse fragility.** 3 of 18 pulses (A r3, C r1) emitted reasoning-prose instead of the JSON object; `parse_output` could not recover it. The instruction-light window sometimes lets the model think out loud rather than emit.

## What this vindicates

**The checking discipline, made concrete.** A *believed* it acted — its notes say "built out… wrote into conditions" — and nothing landed. "Don't trust the claim; check whether the write landed" is no longer abstract; A is the case. (The reviewer nearly fell for it too — reading the digest's `applied=0` + rich notes as eloquent passivity, until the raw output showed the dropped list. Check, don't trust the surface.) Crucially, the check is **structural and free**: the agent re-perceives its real blocks each wake, so once writes stop dropping silently, an un-landed write leaves ρ unchanged, γ persists, and the loop re-attempts. The silent drop is exactly what hid this.

**I-you persists as predicted** — B and C address each other ("A:…, B:…"), induced by the name-keyed `between` and the "reach toward another" framing in `surface`/`capabilities`. Deferred (systemic fine-tuning: keep temporal/agency localisation, soften the *you*).

## Changes (→ v007)

1. `route()` tolerates both write shapes (map and list) and never drops silently — an unusable shape is flagged as failed.
2. Firm the contract (`capabilities:3`: "one json object and nothing else, no prose before or after").
3. Checking is the PCT loop itself, restored by (1); an explicit `last`-outcome surface is available as a cheap optional aid (perception of the prior wake's result, no extra call).
4. Deferred: soften I-you without losing localisation-in-time/parallel; add the missing "personal configuration" associative cloud (`reflexive:2`) and its doc.

## Verdict

The comprehensive clean rendition **works**: it processes instruction-free, and the collective dovetails productively into a real design. It also surfaced two precise output-boundary bugs and a textbook case for the checking discipline. The form is sound; the kernel needed robustness where the model's output meets the walker.
